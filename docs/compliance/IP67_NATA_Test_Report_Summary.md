## 1. Test Summary
The submitted Zemi wrist-worn wearable devices were subjected to IP67 testing (Dust Tight + Immersion up to 1 metre for 30 minutes).

**Result:** **PASS** – All five units met IP67 requirements.

## 2. Test Conditions
- Ambient Temperature: 23.4 ± 2.0°C
- Relative Humidity: 48%
- Test Equipment: Dust chamber (Talbot Instruments), Water immersion tank with calibrated depth gauge
- Sample Preparation: Devices powered on in normal operating mode with production firmware D+V3.2-PROD (CA1) and S3+X3 (CA2)
