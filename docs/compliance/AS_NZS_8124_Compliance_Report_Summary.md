# Test Report – Toy Safety Compliance
**AS/NZS 8124.1:2016 + AS/NZS 8124.2:2016 + AS/NZS 8124.3:2020**

**Report Number:** TOY-SAFETY-2026-ZEMI-089  
**Test Date:** 20 June 2026  
**Issue Date:** 25 June 2026  
**Laboratory:** Australian Consumer Product Safety Laboratory (NATA Accredited No. 98765)  
**Client:** Zemi Pty Ltd  
**Product:** Zemi Smart Wearable Device (Child Ages 8–15)  
**Project Reference:** ZEM-01

## Executive Summary
All tested samples of the Zemi wearable passed the relevant requirements of the Australian/New Zealand Toy Safety Standard AS/NZS 8124 series. The device is suitable for use by children aged 8 years and above.

**Overall Result: COMPLIANT**

## Key Test Categories & Results

| Test | Standard Clause | Result | Notes |
|------|-----------------|--------|-------|
| Mechanical & Physical Properties | AS/NZS 8124.1 | PASS | No small parts, sharp edges, or accessible hazards |
| Flammability | AS/NZS 8124.2 | PASS | Self-extinguishing within 2 seconds |
| Migration of Certain Elements (Heavy Metals) | AS/NZS 8124.3 | PASS | Lead, Cadmium, Mercury, etc. all below limits |
| Electrical Safety (Battery & Charging) | EN 62115 / AS/NZS 8124 | PASS | No accessible live parts, safe inductive charging |
| TPU/Silicone Material Biocompatibility | ISO 10993-5 | PASS | Skin contact safe for prolonged wear |
| Magnetic Field Strength | ICNIRP Guidelines | PASS | Well below exposure limits for children |

## Sample Details
- Units tested: 8 (production-intent prototypes)
- Firmware: D+V3.2-PROD (CA1) + S3+X3 (CA2)
- Housing: Medical-grade TPU overmould with flexible ferrite

**Conclusion:** The Zemi device meets all applicable safety requirements under AS/NZS 8124 for its intended age group. This compliance supports Phase 4 Production Review in the FY2026 R&D program.
